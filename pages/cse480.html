    Computer Ethics Seminar

    Reading and discussion seminar to discuss the social impact of technology through critical perspectives on ethics, data, and innovation, encouraging students to reflect on their role as tech creators.

    This class helped me better understand how technology affects people and society. Especially in a time where GPTs were being first introduced and were growing in popularity, learned about issues like data bias, privacy, and fairness, and how history and politics shape the way tech is built. Through discussions and readings, I practiced thinking critically, listening to different views, and speaking up about what matters. It made me more aware of the responsibility that comes with creating technology.

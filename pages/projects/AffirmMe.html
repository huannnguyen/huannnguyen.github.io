    Inspiration

    We know how beneficial positive daily affirmations are for mental health, confidence, and motivation. But we also know how easy it is to fall out of healthy routines. AffirmMe was designed to be a supportive companion, reinforcing consistency and the power of saying affirming words out loud.

    What It Does

    AffirmMe guides users through scheduled affirmations using text-to-speech, then listens and confirms the user‚Äôs spoken response with voice recognition ‚Äî creating a truly interactive and accountable affirmation practice.

    How We Built It

    We used Flutter and Dart to build a clean and fast mobile interface. Text-to-speech was implemented using built-in libraries, and we extended functionality through an Alexa Skill to support voice-only interaction.

    Challenges We Ran Into

    Setting up Flutter environments and mobile emulators was extremely time-consuming, and syncing separate Git commits across devices caused major delays ‚Äî consuming nearly half our hackathon time.

    Accomplishments We're Proud Of

    We learned a completely new language and framework in a short period, implemented core functionality from scratch, and gained confidence tackling unfamiliar technologies during a fast-paced hackathon.

    What We Learned

    As first-time hackers, we learned to better estimate learning curves, manage time, organize members, and coordinate changes on GitHub.

    What's Next for AffirmMe

    Future plans include completing the full voice-recognition flow, allowing users to select affirmation categories based on personal goals, adding customizable schedules, improving UI/UX, and expanding multi-platform support. Inspired by our Alexa integration, we believe the potential for AffirmMe is far-reaching and the project has only just begun.

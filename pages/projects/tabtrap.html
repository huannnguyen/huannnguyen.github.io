    TabTrap

    A Smart Chrome Extension for Managing Tabs with AI

    TabTrap helps users find and organize browser tabs effortlessly. With fuzzy search and AI-powered grouping, it streamlines workflows, boosts productivity, and keeps tab chaos under control.

    Inspiration

    We were tired of losing track of tabs while researching, doing homework, or working across multiple projects. TabTrap was designed to solve the frustration of managing too many open tabs.

    What It Does

    TabTrap searches through all open tabs and browser history using fuzzy search, and automatically groups tabs by topic with AI. Users can quickly locate the tab they‚Äôre thinking of and stay organized.

    How We Built It

    Built as a Chrome extension with JavaScript. Fuse.js handles fuzzy search, and Google Gemini AI powers automatic tab grouping.

    Challenges We Ran Into

    Switching tab focus, searching browser history efficiently, integrating Fuse.js, and implementing AI tab grouping all required careful handling.

    Accomplishments We're Proud Of

    We successfully built a functional Chrome extension without prior experience, providing a tool that solves a real workflow problem for ourselves and others.

    What We Learned

    Learned to develop Chrome extensions, handle background scripts, manage permissions, and design features that balance functionality, privacy, and simplicity.

    What's Next for TabTrap

    Improve AI tab grouping with adjustable weights and suggestions, and enhance workflow efficiency for all users.
